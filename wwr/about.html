RaftingThrills

At RaftingThrills, our purpose is to deliver unforgettable and safe white water rafting adventures that connect adventurers, families, and teams with the beauty of nature. Our mission is to be a leader in adventure tourism, providing high-quality, adrenaline-packed trips while upholding safety, environmental responsibility, and exceptional customer service. Our creed, 'Adventure Awaits You!', encapsulates our belief in the power of nature to inspire, challenge, and rejuvenate. Our simple yet powerful motto, 'Unleash Your Inner Adventurer,' invites you to break free from the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary journey of white water rafting with us.

History

RaftingThrills, founded by Andre Mendes Pereira, is a company that marries the thrill of white water rafting with the beauty of nature. With a mission to provide safe yet exhilarating rafting adventures, RaftingThrills caters to adventure enthusiasts, families seeking wholesome outdoor fun, and corporate teams in search of unique team-building experiences. Their commitment to fostering camaraderie, personal growth, and environmental appreciation has made them a premier destination for those seeking unforgettable outdoor adventures.
